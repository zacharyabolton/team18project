Facilities at the park

Our world class sensory space is located just to the left of the main entrance and is well sign-posted from any direction in the park.

For accessibility arrangements please contact our office on (087-real-phone-number) and we will be happy to make accommodations for guests with further needs.

Parking available year-round, please see our Tickets Page for the latest offers.

ATMs available at all stores and food services, at the entrance to the park and by the main office.
